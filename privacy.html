Privacy Policy Overview

Last updated: January 15, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how Rowhni ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Islamic companion mobile application ("Service"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information.

Our Core Privacy Principle

Your personal data belongs to you. We design our features to keep your information on your device whenever possible, and we never sell or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

Account Information: If you choose to create an account using Sign in with Apple, we receive only a unique identifier and optionally your email address (if you choose to share it).
App Preferences: Your chosen prayer calculation method, notification settings, and other app customizations.
Dhikr Data: Your dhikr counts, sessions, and progress tracking (stored locally and optionally synced via iCloud).
Feedback: Any feedback, support requests, or communication you send to us.

Information Automatically Collected

Location Data: Your approximate location to calculate accurate prayer times (processed on your device only).
Device Information: Device type, iOS version, and app version for technical support and app optimization.
Usage Analytics: Anonymous usage patterns to help us improve the app (no personal information included).
Crash Reports: Technical data when the app encounters errors (no personal content included).

How We Use Your Information

Essential App Functions

We use your location to calculate accurate prayer times and Qibla direction. This processing happens entirely on your device.

Personalization

Your preferences and settings are stored to provide you with a customized Islamic companion experience.

Data Synchronization

When signed in with Apple ID, your data syncs securely across your devices via iCloud using end-to-end encryption.

App Improvement

Anonymous analytics help us understand how users interact with Rowhni so we can make improvements and fix issues.

Customer Support

We use contact information you provide to respond to your support requests and feedback.

Legal Compliance

We may process information as required by applicable laws, regulations, or legal proceedings.

Data Storage & Security

On-Device Storage

Most of your data, including dhikr counts, preferences, and downloaded Islamic content, is stored securely on your device using iOS encryption.

iCloud Sync

When you sign in with your Apple ID, data syncs through Apple's iCloud service using their industry-standard encryption and security measures.

Voice Processing

Our voice recognition feature for Tasbih counting processes all audio locally on your device using Apple's Core ML framework. No voice data is transmitted to external servers or stored permanently.

Data Encryption

All data transmission is encrypted using TLS/SSL protocols. Data at rest is protected using iOS security features and Apple's encryption standards.

Our Security Commitment

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We continuously work to improve our security practices.

Your Privacy Rights

Access Your Data

You can view and manage your data directly within the Rowhni app settings. Most of your data is stored locally on your device.

Control Data Sharing

You can control what data is synced to iCloud and manage your Sign in with Apple preferences through iOS Settings.

Delete Your Data

You can delete your local app data by deleting the app. iCloud data can be deleted through your Apple ID settings.

Opt-Out of Analytics

You can disable analytics sharing in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Analytics & Improvements.

Location Services

You can control location access for Rowhni in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services.

Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions or requests, contact us through App Store Support or our support channels.
