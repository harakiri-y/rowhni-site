Privacy Policy

Last updated: 31 Aug 2025

1) Controller

Rowhni (“we”, “our”, “us”). Contact: support@rowhni.app. This policy explains what personal data we process and why.

2) Categories of data

  • Account: Sign in with Apple (anonymous identifier; name/email only if you consent). Stored locally and in Apple iCloud/CloudKit for sync.
  • Prayer & Qibla: Approximate location to calculate times/direction. We do not track or sell location.
  • Notifications & Widgets: Preferences are kept locally/App Group.
  • AI “Ask Rowhni”: Your prompts and optional voice transcripts are sent to our AI provider (OpenAI API) to generate answers. Please avoid personal data. See OpenAI privacy: openai.com/policies/privacy-policy.
  • Diagnostics: Apple crash logs and basic analytics if enabled on your device.
  • Payments: Apple In‑App Purchases; we never see your full payment details.

3) Purposes & legal bases (GDPR/UK‑GDPR)

  • Provide core features (prayer times, Qibla, widgets) – Art. 6(1)(b) contract.
  • Optional features (location, notifications, speech recognition) – Art. 6(1)(a) consent.
  • Security, abuse prevention, service quality – Art. 6(1)(f) legitimate interests.
  • Payments and accounting – Art. 6(1)(c) legal obligation and (b).

4) Recipients & processors

  • Apple (iCloud/CloudKit, StoreKit, Notifications) – data hosting & purchases.
  • OpenAI (AI responses) – processing of prompts to generate answers.
  • Hosting for these public pages (GitHub Pages/Netlify/Vercel, as applicable).

We do not sell personal data.

5) International transfers

When data is processed outside your jurisdiction, we rely on appropriate safeguards (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses, Data Privacy Framework participation where applicable).

6) Retention

We keep data only as long as needed. You can delete local data in the app; cloud data follows Apple policies.

7) Your rights

GDPR rights: access, rectification, deletion, restriction, portability, and objection. Contact: support@rowhni.app

You can also lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

8) Children

General audience. Parents/guardians can manage permissions on the device.

9) Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures. No method is 100% secure.

10) Religious disclaimer

The AI assistant is for educational guidance only and is not a fatwa. Please consult qualified scholars for legal rulings.

11) Changes

We may update this policy; the latest version is always available here.

12) Contact

Email: support@rowhni.app
