Voice Tasbih

Learn how to use our revolutionary voice-powered dhikr counting feature.

How Voice Recognition Works

Simply tap the voice button and start reciting your dhikr. Our AI automatically counts SubhanAllah, Alhamdulillah, and Allahu Akbar with 99.7% accuracy.

Supported Languages

Voice recognition works with Arabic dhikr and transliteration in multiple languages and dialects. The system learns and adapts to your voice.

Privacy & Security

Voice processing happens entirely on-device using Apple's Core ML framework. No audio data is transmitted to external servers.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Rowhni free to use?

+

Yes, Rowhni is free to download and includes all essential Islamic features including voice Tasbih, prayer times, Qibla compass, and Quran reading with progress tracking.

How accurate are the prayer times?

+

Our prayer times use precise astronomical calculations with ±1 minute accuracy based on your exact GPS location and selected calculation method from 12+ available options.

Does the app work offline?

+

Yes, prayer times, Qibla direction, voice Tasbih, and most features work offline. An internet connection is only needed for initial setup and content updates.

Is my voice data private?

+

Absolutely. All voice processing happens locally on your device using Apple's Core ML framework. No voice data is transmitted to external servers or stored permanently.

How does voice Tasbih recognition work?

+

Our AI engine recognizes Arabic dhikr and transliteration with 99.7% accuracy across diverse dialects. Simply speak naturally - SubhanAllah, Alhamdulillah, or Allahu Akbar.

Can I sync my progress across devices?

+

Yes, sign in with your Apple ID to securely sync your dhikr counts, Quran progress, and preferences across all your iOS devices via iCloud with end-to-end encryption.
