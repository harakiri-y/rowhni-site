Voice Tasbih

Learn how to use our revolutionary voice-powered dhikr counting feature.

How Voice Recognition Works

Simply tap the voice button and start reciting your dhikr. Our AI will automatically count as you speak - no need to tap manually.

Supported Languages

Voice recognition works with Arabic dhikr pronunciation in multiple languages and dialects. The system learns and adapts to your voice.

Privacy & Security

Voice processing happens on-device for your privacy. No audio data is sent to external servers or stored permanently.

Qibla Direction

Find the accurate direction to Mecca from anywhere in the world.

Compass Calibration

For best accuracy, calibrate your device's compass by moving it in a figure-8 motion when prompted by the app.

Magnetic Interference

Keep your device away from magnetic objects like speakers, magnets, or metal surfaces for accurate readings.

Location Requirements

Qibla direction requires location services to calculate the precise bearing to Mecca from your current position.

Troubleshooting

Common issues and solutions to keep Rowhni running smoothly.

App Not Responding

Try force-closing the app and reopening it. If issues persist, restart your device or reinstall the app from the App Store.

Notification Issues

Check that notifications are enabled in iOS Settings > Notifications > Rowhni. Also verify Do Not Disturb settings.

Voice Recognition Problems

Ensure microphone permissions are granted and you're in a quiet environment. Speak clearly and at a moderate pace.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Rowhni free to use?

Yes, Rowhni is free to download and includes all essential Islamic features. Some advanced features may require Rowhni Pro.

How accurate are the prayer times?

Our prayer times use precise astronomical calculations based on your exact GPS location and are accurate to within minutes.

Does the app work offline?

Yes, prayer times, Qibla direction, and most features work offline. An internet connection is only needed for initial setup and updates.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. Your personal data stays on your device and in your iCloud account. We don't collect or share your personal information.

Can I customize prayer time calculations?

Yes, you can choose from multiple calculation methods and manually adjust times to match your local masjid if needed.

How does voice Tasbih work?

Our AI listens to your dhikr recitation and automatically counts repetitions. All processing happens on your device for privacy.
