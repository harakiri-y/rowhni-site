Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about Rowhni

Prayer Times & Methods

How accurate are the prayer times?

Rowhni uses multiple calculation methods including Muslim World League, Islamic Society of North America, and others. The times are calculated based on your exact location using GPS coordinates for maximum accuracy.

Can I change the calculation method?

Yes! Go to Settings > Prayer Times > Calculation Method to choose from various methods including MWL, ISNA, Egyptian, Makkah, and more.

How do I set my location?

Rowhni automatically detects your location using GPS. You can also manually set your location in Settings > Location. The app will use your current location for accurate prayer times.

Qibla Calibration

How do I calibrate the compass?

Move your device in a figure-8 pattern to calibrate the compass. This helps eliminate magnetic interference and ensures accurate Qibla direction. The app will guide you through the calibration process.

Why is the Qibla direction not accurate?

Magnetic interference from nearby metal objects, electronic devices, or buildings can affect compass accuracy. Try moving to an open area and recalibrate the compass for better results.

Widgets & Notifications

How do I add widgets to my home screen?

Long press on your home screen, tap the "+" button, search for "Rowhni", and choose from different widget sizes. You can display prayer times, Qibla direction, or both.

Can I customize notification timing?

Yes! Go to Settings > Notifications to set custom reminder times before each prayer. You can set different times for each prayer and choose notification sounds.

AI Assistant "Ask Rowhni"

What can I ask the AI assistant?

You can ask about Islamic practices, Quran interpretations, prayer guidance, Islamic history, and general spiritual advice. The AI is trained on authentic Islamic sources.

Is the AI assistant available offline?

No, the AI assistant requires an internet connection to provide accurate and up-to-date responses. Basic prayer times and Qibla features work offline.

In-App Purchases & Restore

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to Settings > Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase. All your premium features will be restored automatically.

What premium features are available?

Premium features include unlimited AI conversations, advanced widgets, custom notification sounds, prayer time adjustments, and priority support.
