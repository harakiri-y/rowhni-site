Getting Started

Essential setup guide for first-time users and app configuration.

Initial Setup Process

Step 1: Download from App Store (iOS 14.0+ required)

Step 2: Grant location permission for prayer time accuracy

Step 3: Select your preferred calculation method and madhab

Step 4: Configure notification preferences and privacy settings

Location & Privacy Settings

Precise Location: Required for accurate prayer times and Qibla direction

Data Processing: Location data processed locally on device only

Manual Override: Set location manually if automatic detection fails

Privacy Controls: Manage all permissions in iOS Settings > Privacy

Troubleshooting Setup Issues

If you experience setup problems, restart the app and ensure iOS is updated. For persistent issues, contact support through the App Store "Report a Problem" feature with your device model and iOS version.

Accuracy Standards & Verification

Precision: ±1 minute accuracy using astronomical calculations based on GPS coordinates

Voice-Powered Dhikr Counter

Revolutionary voice recognition technology for accurate dhikr counting with complete privacy protection.

Supported Dhikr & Recognition Accuracy

Recognized Phrases:

  • SubhanAllah (سبحان الله) - 99.8% accuracy
  • Alhamdulillahi Rabbil Alameen (الحمد لله رب العالمين) - 99.5% accuracy
  • Allahu Akbar (الله أكبر) - 99.7% accuracy
  • La ilaha illa Allah (لا إله إلا الله) - 99.6% accuracy

Languages: Arabic, English transliteration, and mixed pronunciation supported

Privacy & Technical Implementation

On-Device Processing: Voice data never leaves your iPhone - processed using Apple's Core ML framework

No Recording: Audio is analyzed in real-time and immediately discarded

No Internet Required: Voice recognition works completely offline

Battery Optimization: Advanced algorithms minimize battery drain during extended sessions

Usage Tips & Troubleshooting

Optimal Performance: Speak clearly in quiet environment, hold device 6-12 inches away

Microphone Issues: Ensure microphone permissions enabled in Settings > Privacy & Security

Recognition Problems: Try different pronunciations or speak slower/faster

Reset Counter: Long-press counter to reset or access session history

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Rowhni completely free? Are there any hidden costs?

+

Core Features Free: Essential Islamic features including prayer times, Qibla compass, basic dhikr counter, and Quran reading are completely free.

Premium Features: Rowhni Pro subscription unlocks advanced features like voice-powered dhikr counting, additional adhan voices, premium themes, and detailed analytics.

Advertisement Support: The free version is supported by privacy-respecting advertisements. No personal data is collected for ad targeting - ads are contextual only.

No Hidden Costs: Clear pricing for premium features with no surprise charges or mandatory subscriptions.

How can I verify prayer time accuracy and what if times seem wrong?

+

Accuracy Standard: ±1 minute precision using astronomical calculations based on exact GPS coordinates.

Verification Method: Compare with your local mosque times for the first week, adjusting calculation method if needed.

Common Issues: Wrong calculation method selected, inaccurate location detection, or timezone problems.

Troubleshooting: Verify location permissions, check calculation method matches local standard, ensure device timezone is correct.

Professional Validation: Our algorithms are validated against official Islamic calendar calculations used by major Islamic institutions.

What features work offline and what requires internet?

+

Fully Offline Features:

  • Prayer time calculations (once location is set)
  • Qibla direction using device compass
  • Voice Tasbih recognition and counting
  • Quran reading with downloaded translations
  • Progress tracking and statistics

Internet Required For:

  • Initial location detection and setup
  • Downloading new translation languages
  • Streaming audio recitations (if not downloaded)
  • App updates and new feature releases

How is my privacy protected, especially for voice data?

+

Voice Data Privacy:

  • Processing happens entirely on your device using Apple's Core ML
  • No audio data transmitted to servers or stored permanently
  • Voice analysis occurs in real-time and data is immediately discarded
  • No recording, no storage, no cloud processing of voice data

General Privacy:

  • Location data stays on your device for prayer time calculations
  • No personal data shared with third parties
  • No behavioral tracking or advertising profiles created
  • Full privacy policy available with detailed legal protections
